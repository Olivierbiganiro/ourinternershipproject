Nyamagabe Yacu

Nyamagabe ni Akarere gaherereye mu Ntara y'Amajyepfo y'u Rwanda. Nyamagabe iri hagati y'Uturere twa Huye na Nyamasheke mu Majyepfo y'Uburengerazuba bw'u Rwanda. Aka Karere kagizwe n'ibice by'icyahoze ari Intara ya Gikongoro yakuweho mu 2006. Akarere ka Nyamagabe kagizwe n'imirenge 17 ariyo: Buruhukiro, Cyanika, Gatare, Kaduha, Kamegeri, Kibirizi, Kibumbwe, Kitabi, Mbazi, Mugano, Musange, Musebeya, Mushubi, Nkomane, Gasaka, Tare na Uwinkingi.
